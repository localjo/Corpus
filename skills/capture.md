# capture

## Purpose

Add new source material into `raw/` without mutating `wiki/` directly.

## Rules

- Write new captures into `raw/` using stable, descriptive filenames.
- Preserve source text fidelity; do not silently summarize in capture step.
- Do not modify manifest or wiki pages during capture.
- Keep capture append-oriented and low-friction.

## Follow-up

- Ingest updates `wiki/` and `manifest.json`.
